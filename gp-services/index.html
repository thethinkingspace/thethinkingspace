GP Services

  • Referral Pathway
  • Provider of CPD
  • We offer cognitive behavioural psychotherapy, Acceptance and commitment therapy, and Functional Analytic Psychotherapy to suit patients’ presenting issues.
  • We can provide assessment appointments within 7 days of referral.
  • We always provide our referrers with an assessment report with treatment recommendations and keep the medical practitioner informed of progress and end of therapy.

If the patient is on psychotropic medication, we liaise closely with the medical practitioner concerning any changes. Our aim is to increase our patients’ psychological resilience in order to work towards reducing their need for anti-depressants or anti-anxiolytics.
