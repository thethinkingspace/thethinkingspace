The Thinking Space believes in ethical care:

  1. Our Ethical relationship with patients entails ensuring their safety in treatment, providing the most appropriate treatment according to clients' problems, setting realistic targets and knowing when to complete treatments.
  2. We receive regular case supervision and attend seminars, conferences and regular training to ensure their continuing professional development.
  3. We encourage your feedback, with questionnaires before and after treatment. This ensures the constant improvement and development of care plans and our practice as a whole. A complaints procedure is available if any concerns arise that cannot be resolved with the treating practitioner.
