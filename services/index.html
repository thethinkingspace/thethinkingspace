We are friendly, approachable and compassionate. We have regular confidential clinical supervision, so you, our client, will benefit from value added, creative care of the very best quality.

We allow you to stay in control of your treatment by:

  • Always clearly explaining our therapeutic methods
  • Regularly reviewing and assessing progress
  • Emphasizing client learning and active participation in therapy

Our aim is to bring you back to a state of wellbeing and emotional self-sufficiency, fortified with coping skills, a heightened awareness of the connections between past learning, actions, emotions and environment.
