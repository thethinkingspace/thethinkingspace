Dr Marc Mandell - Consultant Psychiatrist

BSc Psych, MB.BS, MRCPsych

Dr Mandell is the medical director at The Thinking Space. Trained at St Bartholomew's Medical School in London, Dr Mandell has been fully registered with the General Medical Council since 1998, and a member of the Royal College of Psychiatrists since 2002. He specialises in both General Adult and Old Age psychiatry and continues to work full time in the NHS. At The Thinking Space, Dr Mandell is available for mental health assessments and can provide medical treatments, as well as medico-legal assessments. He offers medical input to our therapy team to formulate the best care plan for each client.

He is fully accredited in general adult and old age psychiatry.
