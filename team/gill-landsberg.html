Gill Landsberg - Psychotherapist

MSc

Gill is an Integrative Psychotherapist with a Masters and Advanced Diploma from Regent's University, London. Gill draws on a number of therapeutic approaches, tailored to suit the individual needs of her clients. She has worked in a number of clinical settings including private practice and the NHS. Her earlier career in corporate management provides her with valuable experience of the challenges of juggling the demands of work, relationships and family in today's world. Accredited by the UKCP and a member of the BACP, Gill has experience of working with an extensive range of problems including stress, anxiety, depression, bereavement and relationship issues.
