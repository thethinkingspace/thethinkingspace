Gemma Mead - Cognitive Behavioural Therapist

BA (Hons), BSc (Hons) RMN, PgDip CBT, Cert MBCT

Gemma is a Cognitive Behavioural Therapist, Mental Health Nurse and a Mindfulness Practitioner (Mindfulness –Based Cognitive Therapy). Gemma has a rich and varied history as a clinician and therapist, supporting people and their loved ones in their recovery from mental health conditions/emotional problems. Since qualifying twelve years ago, Gemma’s journey began in acute in-patient hospitals working with people with mental health and drug/alcohol problems, then moving to the community as a senior clinician in mental health teams, providing individual psychological interventions, group therapy and workshops. She has worked as a Senior Practitioner Trainer within the NHS and voluntary sector, designing and delivering training to doctors, mental health professionals, business, education and people experiencing mental health conditions and their families.

Gemma presently works in several London Universities supporting students with their mental health and people on the Autistic Spectrum and those with Asperger’s. Coupled with this she is a Consultant Trainer for doctors, mental health professionals and business on a range of CBT, Mindfulness and Recovery courses across the UK and Europe.
